Three cats inside a tiger's den

"Beom is the first cat that came to our house among the three cats. Beom's breed is called 'Korat', which is known as a lucky cat. Since Beom came to our house, many interesting things have happened in our life. He often sleeps right next to me when he sleeps"

Beom, 7years old

"Sans is a cat that I personally brought from the street. She is the only female among the cats and has a very gentle personality. She has a short tail, which is her attractive point. Just looking at her makes my heart ache."

Sands, 7years old

"Hoya is the youngest among the three cats, but he is the largest in size. These days, people call him the "domestic tiger" because he weighs almost 8kg. I love his big size, but he has a timid personality, which makes him funny to watch. Hoya loves to play the most."

Hoya, 5years old
